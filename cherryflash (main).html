who are we?

Made for little memories

CherryFlash is a student-launched camera rental project designed for girls who want an easier and prettier way to capture everyday moments. We created it because digital cameras are trendy again, but the rental experience often still feels too technical, too cold, or too inconvenient.

Our goal is to offer a softer alternative: curated cameras, simple rentals, and a brand that helps people keep the feeling of a day through photos that feel personal, warm, and real.
