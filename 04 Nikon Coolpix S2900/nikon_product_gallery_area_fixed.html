Guidelines of use
Please handle the camera with care during the full rental period.

• Keep the camera in its pouch or case when not in use.
• Avoid dropping it or placing it on unstable surfaces.
• Do not expose it to rain, water, sand, or excessive heat.
• Use clean hands when handling the lens and screen.
• Do not force the battery, memory card, or lens cover.
• Return the camera with all included items and accessories.

Damage caused by improper handling may result in an additional charge. We kindly ask every customer to treat each camera as a shared memory item for the next person too.
